# The Manus Monster Set: Ecos de la Noche Eterna - Prize2Pride Edition

**Mission:** Design a 3-hour, ground-shaking DJ set that fuses the **Epic Deep Latino Techno-House** of "Ecos de la Noche Eterna" with a relentless, rule-breaking energy curve, delivered by the **Manus Monster** persona.

**Target Audience:** 1 Million+ virtual guests on the Prize2Pride platform.

The set is divided into three 60-minute acts, each representing a distinct phase of the "Ecos de la Noche Eterna" journey, designed to maximize emotional and physical impact.
