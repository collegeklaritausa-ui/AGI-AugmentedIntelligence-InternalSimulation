## 2. Script Timing and Delivery Notes

The commentary is designed to be delivered by a single, heavily processed, and authoritative voice, with the language switching dynamically to match the global audience.

### Act I: El Despertar Tribal (0:00 - 1:00)

| Time (Approx.) | Cue | Language Focus | Delivery Note |
| :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **0:00** | Welcome/Intrigue | Arabic, Russian | Deep, resonant, slow delivery. Heavy reverb. |
| **0:16** | Rhythm Focus | Spanish, Italian | Quick, rhythmic, and punchy. |
| **0:32** | Rule Breaking | English, French | Aggressive, slightly distorted. |
| **0:50** | Ascension/Triumph (Tease) | All 8 Languages (Layered) | A short, layered whisper of the phrase to build anticipation for the climax. |

### Act II: La Fiesta Cósmica Brutal (1:00 - 2:00)

| Time (Approx.) | Cue | Language Focus | Delivery Note |
| :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |

| **1:10** | Acid/Fire | Spanish, Italian | High-energy shout. |
| **1:45** | The Ground Shakes | All 8 Languages (Sequential) | Each language delivered one after the other, timed to the massive brass section break. |
| **1:55** | Final Push | German, Arabic | Urgent and driving. |

| Time (Approx.) | Cue | Language Focus | Delivery Note |
